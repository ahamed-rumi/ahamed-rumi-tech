How to change wallpaper in windows

In order to change the wallpaper, go to settings, or press windows + i on your keyboard, then either search for background, or go to the personalization tab and change the wallpaper!

How to hibernate your PC

in some computers you may not see the option for hibernating your PC, in such situations, go to the control panel, search for "power" and select power options. Go to choos what the power button does, and click change options that are currently unavailable and check hibernation. Now, go to the power menu, and there should be an option for hibernation

How to turn off fast-startup

Turning off fast-startup makes your computer entirely turn off when shutting down. In order to turn off fast-startup, go to control panel, go to power options, click choose what the power button does, and then click change settings that are currently unavailable, and uncheck fast startup. Now, your PC turns of completely after shutting down.
